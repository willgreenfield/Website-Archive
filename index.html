Current Projects

FPV Quadcopter 

Lets make a capable yet affordable FPV drone! The goal here is to build a functional quadcopter that will be a great base for exploring AI and neural networks. Later we will design and fit a sensor suite for environmental mapping and navigation.

Soft-Robotics Gripping Arm

My first successful prototype of a pneumatic soft-robotic gripper arm made from everyday materials and a 3D printed mold. Air is injected into the silicon arm causing the joints to inflate, thus bending the arm in a claw-like motion. A video of this project in action is posted on my Instagram account.
